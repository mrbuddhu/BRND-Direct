Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a wholesale order on BRND Direct?
To place a wholesale order: (1) Browse the Product Catalogue and click "Add to Order". (2) Review your order in the cart. (3) Select your payment method (Net 30, wire transfer, or trade finance). (4) Confirm delivery address and shipping preferences. (5) Submit your order — you'll receive email confirmation instantly.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ varies by supplier and product. You'll find the MOQ clearly listed on each product card. Typically, MOQs range from 12 units for accessories to 50+ units for apparel. Some verified suppliers offer lower MOQs for first-time orders.
What payment methods are accepted?
BRND Direct accepts: Net 30/60/90 terms (for verified buyers), Wire Transfer (domestic and international), Letters of Credit (for large orders), Trade Finance via our partner lenders, and credit/debit cards for orders under $10,000.
How does Trade Finance work on the platform?
Our Trade Finance feature allows verified buyers to apply for purchase order financing. Simply submit a quote, get approved (usually within 24-48 hours), and we fund the supplier on your behalf. You repay on agreed terms (30/60/90 days). Rates start at 2.5% per month.
How do I track my order shipment?
Go to Orders → find your order → click "Track Shipment". You'll see real-time carrier updates. Tracking numbers are typically available within 24 hours of dispatch. You'll also receive automated email/SMS notifications at key milestones.
How do I get my account verified?
Account verification requires: (1) Valid business registration documents, (2) Tax ID / EIN, (3) Trade references (2-3), (4) Bank verification. Upload these in Account → Business Details → Verification. Verification typically takes 2-3 business days.
Can I add team members to my account?
Yes! Go to Account → Settings → Team Members. You can invite team members by email and assign roles: Admin (full access), Buyer (can place orders), Finance (can view/pay invoices), or Viewer (read-only). Team member seats are included in all plans.
What is the dispute resolution process?
If you receive an incorrect, damaged, or missing order: (1) Go to Orders → select the order → "Raise Dispute". (2) Provide evidence (photos, descriptions). (3) BRND Direct mediates between you and the supplier within 5 business days. (4) Resolutions include replacement, partial refund, or full refund.
How are invoices generated and sent?
Invoices are automatically generated upon order confirmation. You can view and download all invoices from the Invoices & Payments section. Invoices are also emailed to your registered address. Custom invoice templates and bulk export (CSV/PDF) are available.
